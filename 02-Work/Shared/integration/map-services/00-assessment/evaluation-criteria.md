# Vendor Evaluation Criteria

## Scoring System

Each criterion is scored from 1-5:
- **5**: Excellent - Exceeds expectations
- **4**: Good - Meets all requirements
- **3**: Adequate - Meets most requirements
- **2**: Poor - Significant gaps
- **1**: Unacceptable - Does not meet requirements

**Total Score**: Sum of all weighted scores (max 100)

## Decision Framework

### Score Interpretation
- **90-100**: Excellent - Highly recommended
- **80-89**: Good - Recommended with minor concerns
- **70-79**: Adequate - Acceptable with reservations
- **60-69**: Poor - Not recommended without improvements
- **< 60**: Unacceptable - Reject

### Final Recommendation Factors
1. Total weighted score
2. Critical requirements met (must-haves)
3. Deal breaker absence
4. Strategic fit with company roadmap
5. Risk assessment
